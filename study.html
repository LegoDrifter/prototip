Details on Pictura and the online course

Fellow art lovers, welcome to Pictura.
This website is launched with the hope of
sharing knowledge and creating a community
of art lovers. We provide various tests
and interactiveness with the system.

The course primarily follows the book provided
"The Usborne Book Of Famous Paintings" written by
Rosie Dickens. Bellow is provided a link to
download the book will be using.

Please mind that you need the knowledge of this book
to complete the tasks on this website and unlock
the trophies in your trophy room!

Good luck
Pictura team
